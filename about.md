# About Me

I'm a senior cloud-native engineer and trainer based in Berlin. I build Kubernetes platforms, design CI/CD pipelines, and help teams move faster without breaking things.

My career started in 1997 as an IT technician in Sakarya, Turkey. Since then, I've worked across the full stack of cloud infrastructure - from Alstom's Marmaray rail project in Istanbul, through cloud operations at HERE Technologies and Vodafone, to senior architecture roles at Docker and Microsoft Germany.

In 2023, I founded **Ayti.Tech**, my own consulting and training practice. I work with organizations to adopt cloud-native technologies, modernize their infrastructure, and build DevOps capabilities.

When I'm not working on platforms, I create educational content - my courses on Udemy have reached over 25,000 students, and my YouTube channel covers cloud-native topics for a Turkish-speaking audience.

I live in Berlin with my wife and two children. I hold certifications in Kubernetes (CKA), Docker (DCA), Azure, and AWS.
